# Case Study — Executive Media Training (Sanitized)

## Situation
A senior leader was scheduled for a high-visibility interview with limited prep time and multiple sensitive topics in play.

## Goal
Deliver clear, consistent messaging; avoid speculation; maintain confidence under fast follow-ups.

## Approach
- Built a one-page message map (core message + proof points)
- Created approved bridging language for predictable hard questions
- Ran rapid mock interviews focused on speed, tone, and clarity
- Tightened soundbites into 30–60 second versions

## Outcome
Leader stayed on-message, reduced overexplaining, and handled follow-ups with calm pivots to key points.
